WendyClippersbot Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how WendyClippersbot collects, uses, stores, and shares information when the bot is used in a Discord server.

1. Information We Collect

WendyClippersbot may collect the following information:

  • Discord user ID and Discord username.
  • Linked social media platform and username.
  • Verification codes generated by the bot.
  • Submitted social media post links.
  • Public post metrics, such as view counts, platform name, tracking status, campaign type, bounty tag, and cycle information.
  • Payment preference details submitted by users, such as PayPal email, first name, and last name.
  • Bot command activity needed to provide, debug, and maintain the service.

2. Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect

WendyClippersbot is not designed to collect passwords, private messages, bank account numbers, credit card numbers, government identification numbers, or tax documents. Do not submit sensitive information through bot commands unless specifically requested by the server operator through an approved payment provider.

3. How We Use Information

Information is used to:

  • Link Discord users with submitted social media accounts.
  • Track submitted post links and public view metrics.
  • Display personal stats, leaderboards, and campaign cycle results.
  • Help administrators review campaigns, detect abuse, and prepare payouts.
  • Maintain, troubleshoot, and improve the bot.

4. Social Media Platform Data

When users submit public social media links, WendyClippersbot may request publicly available information or approved API data from the relevant platform. The availability and type of data depends on each platform's API policies, permissions, and user authorization requirements.

5. Payment Preference Information

WendyClippersbot may store payout preference details, such as PayPal information, so server administrators can review payment methods. This information is used for payout administration only. The bot does not process card payments, bank transfers, tax forms, or identity verification.

6. Who Can See Information

Users can view their own account, payment preference status, and tracked post stats. Authorized server administrators may view user submissions, stats, campaign cycle data, and payment preference status for moderation, campaign management, fraud prevention, and payout review.

7. Data Sharing

WendyClippersbot may share information with:

  • Discord, because the bot operates inside Discord.
  • Social media platforms whose APIs are used to retrieve public metrics or authorized account information.
  • Hosting, database, and infrastructure providers used to operate the bot.
  • Server administrators who manage campaigns and payouts.

We do not sell personal information.

8. Data Retention

Information may be retained while the bot is installed in a server and as needed for campaign records, moderation, payout review, dispute handling, and technical maintenance. Users may ask the server owner or bot operator to remove their stored information where reasonably possible.

9. Security

Reasonable steps are taken to protect bot data, but no system is perfectly secure. Users should avoid submitting unnecessary sensitive information through Discord commands.

10. Children's Privacy

WendyClippersbot is intended for Discord communities that comply with Discord's age requirements and applicable laws. The bot is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children where prohibited by law.

11. Your Choices

You may stop using the bot at any time. Depending on server settings, you may remove linked accounts, remove payment details, or request that a server administrator delete your stored bot data.

12. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the bot after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

13. Contact

For privacy questions or data removal requests, contact the server owner or bot operator through the Discord community where WendyClippersbot is installed.
